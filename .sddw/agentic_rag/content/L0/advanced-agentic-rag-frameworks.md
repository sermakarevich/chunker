# advanced-agentic-rag-frameworks

**Parent:** [[index]]

Advanced agentic frameworks, including Adaptive RAG, Agent-G, GeAR, and ADW, significantly enhance Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) by integrating structured graph data, state management, and autonomous workflows. For instance, the Agentic Document Workflows (ADW) system automates the processing of an invoice (INV-2025-045) by parsing details, retrieving relevant contract terms, and recommending a payment action (for example, securing a 2% discount). Architecturally, these systems utilize components like a Critic Module (found in Agent-G) or a Classifier Role (used in Adaptive RAG) to ensure data quality and dynamic adaptability, providing deep reasoning capabilities that previously surpassed traditional RAG methods.
