# academic-papers-nlp-references

**Parent:** [[index]]

The following list comprises various academic papers, datasets, and models, detailing foundational advancements and specific research areas within natural language processing and AI:

*   **Codesearchnet challenge:** Authors Hamel Husain, Ho-Hsiang Wu, Tiferet Gazit, Miltiadis Allamanis, and Marc Brockschmidt presented this paper for the Codesearchnet challenge, which evaluates the state of semantic code search, published in 2020.
*   **"knowing when you don’t know":** The work by Nandan Thakur, Luiz Bonifacio, Xinyu Zhang, Odunayo Ogundepo, Ehsan Kamalloo, David Alfonso-Hermelo, Xiaoguang Li, Qun Liu, Boxing Chen, Mehdi Rezagholizadeh, and Jimmy Lin introduced a multilingual relevance assessment dataset for robust retrieval-augmented generation, published in 2024.
*   **Pointer sentinel mixture models:** This research, published in 2016, was conducted by Stephen Merity, Caiming Xiong, James Bradbury, and Richard Socher.
*   **Training verifiers to solve math word problems:** This 2021 paper was authored by Karl Cobbe, Vineet Kosaraju, Mohammad Bavarian, Mark Chen, Heewoo Jun, Lukasz Kaiser, Matthias Plappert, Jerry Tworek, Jacob Hilton, Reiichiro Nakano, Christopher Hesse, and John Schulman.
*   **The JRC-Acquis:** This is a multilingual aligned parallel corpus containing over 20 languages, presented by Ralf Steinberger, Bruno Pouliquen, Anna Widiger, Camelia Ignat, Tomaž Erjavec, Dan Tufiş, and Dániel Varga at the Fifth International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC‘06) in Genoa, Italy, in May 2006.
