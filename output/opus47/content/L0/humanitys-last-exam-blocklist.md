In agentic tests, it scored 91.0% on ChartXiv (with tools) and 87.6% on ScreenSpot-Pro (with tools), while demonstrating superior safety improvements, such as an ASR of 2.34% in coding red-teaming, and an achieved final balance of $10,937 in Vending-Bench.

The **Humanity’s Last Exam (HLE)** benchmark assesses model capabilities by employing a blocklist mechanism that functions through substring matching against web URLs. To perform the matching, the system normalizes both the target URLs and the blocklist patterns by removing forward slashes (“/”) and setting both to lowercase. A URL is blocked if any of the normalized blocklist patterns appear as a substring within the normalized URL.
